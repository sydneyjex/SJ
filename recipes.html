Rocky Mountain Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • 2 cups Rice Krispies
  • 1 bag chocolate chips (or ½ chocolate chips, ½ white chips)

Instructions

Cream together sugars and butter. Add eggs and vanilla and beat well. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Add to sugar mixture.

Fold in oats, Rice Krispies, and chocolate chips. Drop by spoonful onto a greased baking sheet. Bake at 350°F for 8–10 minutes.
